My Senate Vote know your senate

Welcome to My Senate Vote. This website will help you prepare your below the line senate vote before the 2016 Australian federal election on 2 July.

The Australian Electoral Commission's instructions for voting below the line are: "If you vote below the line, you must number at least 12 boxes from 1 to 12. Place a 1 in the box beside the candidate that is your first choice, and the numbers 2, 3, 4 and so on to at least the number 12. You can continue to place numbers in the order of your choice in as many boxes below the line as you like."

Using this website is not an alternative to voting. You must vote using one of the methods provided by the AEC.

The numbers you enter on this website will be stored in your local browser. These numbers are not sent anywhere.

To get started, select your state or territory from the navigation menu at the top.
